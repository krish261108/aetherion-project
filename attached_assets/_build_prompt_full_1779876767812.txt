IMPORTANT CONSTRAINTS
I Core Rules
• Do everything in one complete build.
• Do not ask questions — make smart assumptions where details are missing.
• No external APIs. No backend. No database. No login required.
• Use local / static data files or JavaScript objects as the data layer.
• No copyrighted images or assets. No random image URLs that may break.
• Use generated CSS shapes, gradients, SVG symbols, animated particles, glassmorphism cards.
• Must be responsive on mobile, tablet, and desktop.
• Must be deployable directly from Replit.

UNIVERSE CORE
The Aetherion Cycle is a story about a broken reality, the danger of trying to control it, and the journey of
understanding how to bring it back into balance.
Core Concept
The story follows Adrian, a highly observant boy who notices small breaks in reality and discovers that the world is
built on a deeper system called Aetherion — a force that connects both science and magic. As he learns more, he is
pulled into a growing conflict where humans try to control this system while others try to protect its balance.
Themes
Main theme: Control vs Balance
• Knowing something is not the same as understanding it
• Power should not always be used, even if you can
• Too much order or too much chaos both lead to destruction
• A person is defined by choices, not abilities
• Science is not wrong, but incomplete understanding can become dangerous
• Reality cannot be forced into perfection
Conflict Structure
Control: Victor, Arkan, Project AETHER
Balance: Liora, natural system, Aetherion
Understanding: Adrian
Imbalance: Riven, Void, fractured system

Character Progression

WORLD SYSTEM / REALITY LAYERS
Reality is not a single world but a multi-layered system held together by Aetherion. Each layer is part of the same
system, governed by different rules, and separated for stability. Core principle: Reality is stable only when its layers
remain balanced and properly separated.
The Veil
The invisible boundary between layers. Functions: separates different realities, prevents rule conflicts, maintains
stability. Conditions: Stable (worlds separate), Fractured (anomalies occur), Broken (layers merge).
Five Reality Layers
1. Scientific World — Surface Layer
Visible world governed by science and logic. Structured, predictable, limited perception. Cannot naturally access
Aetherion. Starting point and Project AETHER location; represents control and incomplete understanding.
2. Magical World — Hidden Layer
Parallel layer where Aetherion is naturally accessible. Clan-based society, energy-driven interactions, deeper
awareness. Represents balance; introduces the system.
3. Celestial Planes — Origin Layer
Higher-level reality closer to the original Aetherion Core. Less physical, abstract, energy-based. Holds knowledge of
origin. Site of major revelations.
4. Forbidden Dimensions — Broken Layer
Corrupted parts of reality created by imbalance or failed interference. Unstable, hostile, unpredictable. Spatial
distortion, logic distortion, void presence. Consequence of imbalance.
5. Temporal Layer — System Layer
Underlying layer connecting all others through time. Governs sequence, causality, continuity. Unstable behavior:
loops, overlaps, distortions. Critical in later books.
Instability Stages
• Stage 1: small anomalies and visual glitches
• Stage 2: unstable zones and energy distortions
• Stage 3: layer overlap and rule conflict
• Stage 4: time breakdown and spatial distortion
• Stage 5: full convergence and system collapse
Convergence
Controlled convergence = aligned forces and stable system.
Uncontrolled convergence = conflicting forces and destruction.

philosophy, and a way of seeing the world. Master rule: Clans are not defined by power, but by what they believe
about reality.

PEOPLE SYSTEM
People are not equal in how they connect to Aetherion. A person's relationship with Aetherion defines their abilities,
limitations, and place in society. Master rule: Power is not just ability; it is connection, understanding, and cost.
Purebloods
Definition: Individuals fully aligned with a single clan.
Nature: Stable, predictable, deeply connected to one force.
Strengths: Reliability, mastery, clear identity.
Weaknesses: Limited perspective, struggle with opposing forces.
Social View / Note: Respected and considered normal.
Conflux
Definition: Individuals born with connection to two clans.
Nature: Unstable but powerful. Types: balanced, strained, fractured.
Strengths: Versatility, adaptability, high potential.
Weaknesses: Instability, internal conflict, risk of losing control.
Social View / Note: Sometimes feared, sometimes valued.
Nulls
Definition: Individuals with no connection to Aetherion.
Nature: Outside the system entirely.
Strengths: Difficult to detect, unaffected by many abilities, unpredictable.
Weaknesses: No natural defense.
Social View / Note: Hidden importance: the only ones not controlled by the system.
Severed
Definition: Individuals who reject or cannot access Aetherion and turn to science.
Nature: Disconnected from natural system, rely on technology.
Strengths: Intelligence, adaptability, strategy.
Weaknesses: Dependence on technology, unstable artificial power.
Social View / Note: Core belief: power should be built, not inherited.
Veilbreakers
Definition: Extremist group within the Severed helping Project AETHER.
Nature: Ideological, aggressive, willing to betray.
Strengths: Infiltration, sabotage, system manipulation.
Weaknesses: Fully dependent on Project AETHER infrastructure.
Social View / Note: Core belief: the system must be broken to be rebuilt.

PROJECT AETHER — CLASSIFIED
I ACCESS LEVEL: RESTRICTED
Project AETHER is a scientific initiative created to understand, replicate, and ultimately control Aetherion. Core belief:
If reality follows rules, it can be controlled.
Origin
Started as a research program after scientists observed anomalies, unexplained energy patterns, and repeated
irregularities. They detected a structured energy behind reality and concluded that reality was governed by a hidden
system.
Goal Evolution
• Phase 1 — Understanding: study anomalies and collect data.
• Phase 2 — Replication: recreate Aetherion artificially.
• Phase 3 — Control: manipulate energy and influence reality.
• Phase 4 — Domination / Arkan Phase: redesign system and enforce stability.
Key Figures
Victor Voss — Early Leader
Belief: control equals protection.
"If we don't control it, it will destroy us."
Dr. Arkan Virel — Dominant Antagonist
Belief: balance is unreliable; stability must be engineered.
"Balance is unreliable. Stability must be engineered."
Final Master Line
Project AETHER is humanity's attempt to control reality — and the reason reality begins to collapse.

POWER SYSTEM / AETHERION RULES
Aetherion is the hidden system behind reality. Science and magic are two interpretations of the same force. Clans are
living expressions of forces. Artifacts are fragments of the broken system. People are defined by their connection,
rejection, or status outside the system.
Rules of Aetherion
Rule 1: Power requires connection.
Rule 2: Connection requires compatibility.
Rule 3: Every use has cost.
Rule 4: Opposing forces create instability.
Rule 5: Control without balance accelerates collapse.
Rule 6: Artifacts amplify but also destabilize.
Rule 7: The Void is not evil; it is imbalance and entropy.
Rule 8: True stability requires alignment, not domination.
