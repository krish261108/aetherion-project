UNIVERSE CORE:
Include this central franchise definition:

The Aetherion Cycle is a story about a broken reality, the danger of trying to control it, and the journey of understanding how to bring it back into balance.

Core concept:
The story follows Adrian, a highly observant boy who starts noticing small breaks in reality and slowly discovers that the world is built on a deeper system called Aetherion, a force that connects both science and magic. As he learns more, he is pulled into a growing conflict where humans try to control this system while others try to protect its balance.

Main theme:
Control vs Balance

Secondary themes:
- Knowing something is not the same as understanding it
- Power should not always be used, even if you can
- Too much order or too much chaos both lead to destruction
- A person is defined by choices, not abilities
- Science is not wrong, but incomplete understanding can become dangerous
- Reality cannot be forced into perfection

Conflict structure:
- Control: Victor, Arkan, Project AETHER
- Balance: Liora, natural system, Aetherion
- Understanding: Adrian
- Imbalance: Riven, Void, fractured system
